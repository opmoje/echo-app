Instagram Chatbot Demo Application

Last Updated: November 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Instagram Chatbot Demo ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our Instagram chatbot demonstration application.

Important: This is a demonstration application created for testing and educational purposes. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information from Instagram

When you interact with our chatbot through Instagram Direct Messages, we may collect:

  • Instagram User ID: A unique identifier for your Instagram account
  • Message Content: Text messages you send to our Instagram account
  • Timestamp: Date and time of your messages

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical data including API request logs
  • Error logs for debugging purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Echo Functionality: To demonstrate the chatbot by sending back the same message you send
  • Testing & Development: To test and improve the chatbot functionality
  • Debugging: To identify and fix technical issues
We DO NOT:
  • Store your messages in a permanent database
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Sell or rent your personal information

4. Data Storage and Security

Temporary Storage: Your messages are processed in memory only and are not permanently stored. When the application restarts, all message data is cleared.

Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Webhook signature verification
  • Secure storage of API credentials
  • Regular security updates

5. Data Retention

Since this is a demonstration application with in-memory storage, your data is retained only during the active session. All data is automatically cleared when:

  • The application is restarted
  • The server is shut down
  • Memory is cleared

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what data we process
  • Stop Using: Stop interacting with the chatbot at any time
  • Questions: Contact us with any privacy concerns

7. Instagram Integration

This application uses Instagram's Messaging API. Your use of Instagram is governed by Instagram's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing:

  • Instagram Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Terms of Use

8. Children's Privacy

This demonstration application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this Privacy Policy was last revised. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through:

  • GitHub: https://github.com/opmoje/echo-app
  • Instagram: @opmoje

11. Consent

By using our Instagram chatbot, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
